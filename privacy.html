GOD+生 Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 22, 2026

Developer: Bekking Studio (info.bekking@gmail.com)

1. Data Storage

All your data (activity records, plans, books, goals) is stored locally on your device using browser localStorage. No data is collected or sent to our servers.

2. Google Sign-In (Optional)

If you choose to sign in with Google, your data is synced to Google Firebase Firestore under your Google account. This is solely for backup and cross-device sync purposes. We do not access, analyze, or share your synced data.

3. Data We Collect

  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics (page views, feature usage) to improve the app. No personal information is collected.
  • Google Auth: Your email and display name are used only for login display. We do not store or share this information.

4. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect your personal activity records
  • We do not collect your reading notes or book data
  • We do not sell or share any user data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google Firebase: Authentication and cloud storage (optional)
  • Google Analytics: Anonymous usage analytics

6. Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time by clearing your browser data. If you signed in with Google, you can delete your cloud data by logging out and requesting deletion via email.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: info.bekking@gmail.com
